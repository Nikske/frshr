How it works

Our vape juice is extracted from the freshest air our planet has to offer.
To get this air, we work with local farmers at fair prices*. Then our organically* farmed air gets converted to vape juice with science stuff.
After we add our special ingredients™, our products are ready to inhale.
Worried about your carbon footprint? Don't be. All the air that you breathe out can be recycled with our Second Wind™ initiative.
